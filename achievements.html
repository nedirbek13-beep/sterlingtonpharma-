Our Track Record

Built on reliability.

Since 2016, Sterlington has grown from a single pharmacy operation into a recognised B2B pharmaceutical distributor. Our reputation is built not on promises, but on consistent delivery.

Hospital procurement teams and licensed distributors return to us because every shipment arrives with complete documentation, verified quality, and on the timeline we committed to.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped us.

2016

The Beginning

Sterlington was founded in Edinburgh, Scotland, starting as a pharmacy with one commitment: every patient deserves verified, high-quality medicines.

2019

Own Brand Launch

We began manufacturing and distributing under our own brand, expanding from a single operation into a dedicated pharmaceutical supplier.

2022

Central Asia Expansion

Established licensed distribution partnerships across Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan and neighbouring markets.

2026

A Portfolio of 19

Today our portfolio spans 19 injectable, IV infusion and oral suspension products, all sourced from GMP-certified manufacturers.
