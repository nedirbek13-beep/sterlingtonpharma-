Platform Philosophy

A supply platform, not a vendor.

Most distributors are organised around shipments. Sterlington is organised around programmes. Every element of our platform is designed to support healthcare partners as their needs evolve, regulatory expectations increase, and scale becomes unavoidable.

01 — Foundation

Inspection-Ready by Design

Every product is supplied with complete batch records, Certificates of Analysis, and import documentation. We maintain inspection readiness from day one — not as an afterthought.

02 — Process

Discipline Over Throughput

We prioritise verified counterparty checks and rigorous KYC procedures over speed. Every order, every relationship is documented and reviewed before fulfilment.

03 — Infrastructure

Built to Scale Cleanly

Our cold chain logistics and regulatory frameworks are built to expand without disruption — supporting growth across multiple countries and product categories.

04 — Reliability

Long-Term Operating Discipline

We favour long-term reliability over short-term volume. Our partnerships are built across years, not single transactions — with consistent quality at every shipment.

What We Do

Focused sterile injectable supply.

Sterlington focuses exclusively on sterile injectable and oral pharmaceutical supply. We do not chase breadth — we build depth where it matters most. Our portfolio spans antibiotics, antifungals, IV infusions, and oral suspensions, all from verified GMP-certified sources.

A

Injectable Antibiotics

Broad-spectrum and specialist injectable antibiotics including fluoroquinolones, cephalosporins, aminoglycosides and combination preparations — designed for hospital and institutional use.

B

IV Infusion Solutions

Sterile intravenous infusions for hospital settings — including specialist metabolic agents, analgesics, and high-acuity antibiotic infusions delivered in 100ml bags.

C

Oral Suspensions

A full range of paediatric and adult oral antibiotic and antifungal suspensions in 100ml bottles — supporting outpatient and community pharmacy distribution.

D

Documentation & Compliance

Every product is supplied with complete regulatory packages: GMP certificates, batch records, Certificates of Analysis, and full import documentation aligned with UK and international standards.
