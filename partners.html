We do not treat partnerships as transactions. Every distributor, hospital and supplier we work with receives the same standard: transparency, reliability and long-term commitment.

02 — Suppliers

The highest supplier standard

We source exclusively from GMP-certified manufacturers. Every supplier undergoes due diligence before we list a single product.

03 — Institutions

Hospitals served with seriousness

Our Commitment

A relationship built on trust.

When you partner with Sterlington, you are not just placing an order. You are gaining a supply partner who shares your commitment to patient safety and regulatory compliance.

We verify, we document, and we follow through — because the institutions that depend on us cannot afford anything less.

Partnership Process

From first conversation
to active supply.

01

Initial Enquiry

You reach out via our contact details. We respond within one business day with a full introduction and product catalogue tailored to your market.

02

Qualification

We verify your licensing, distribution infrastructure and regulatory standing. This protects both parties and ensures compliant supply.

03

Product Selection

We work with you to identify which products are most relevant to your market, considering therapeutic need and regulatory clearance.

04

Documentation Package

We provide complete batch records, GMP certificates and Certificates of Analysis for your local regulatory submission.

05

First Shipment

Cold chain logistics arranged and tracked from our supplier to your warehouse, with follow-up after delivery.
